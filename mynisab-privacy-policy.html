My Nisab Privacy Policy

My Nisab is a Zakat calculator app for Muslim users.

Data Storage

All wealth data entered by the user is stored locally on the device. No personal financial data is transmitted to any server.

Real-Time Price Data

The app uses public gold and silver price APIs to calculate Nisab thresholds. No personal information is sent.

In-App Purchases

The app uses RevenueCat to process optional Khair Contribution subscriptions.

Notifications

Local notifications remind users of Hawl dates and Zakat obligations.
