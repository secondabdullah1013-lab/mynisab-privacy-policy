## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: What is My Nisab?**
A: My Nisab is a Zakat calculator app designed for Muslim users to easily calculate their annual Zakat obligation based on their wealth portfolio (gold, silver, cash, trade goods, agriculture, and livestock).

**Q: Do I need to create an account to use the app?**
A: No, the app does not require account creation or login. You can start calculating your Zakat immediately after installation.

**Q: Is my financial data safe?**
A: Yes, absolutely. All wealth data you enter is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

### Zakat Calculation

**Q: How does the app calculate Nisab?**
A: The app uses real-time gold and silver price data from a public API to determine the current Nisab threshold and helps you calculate your Zakat obligation based on your wealth.

**Q: What wealth categories does the app support?**
A: The app supports calculations for: gold, silver, cash, trade goods, agriculture, and livestock.

**Q: What is Hawl?**
A: Hawl is the Islamic lunar year (approximately 354-355 days). You're obligated to pay Zakat on wealth you've possessed for a full Hawl.

### Subscriptions & Purchases

**Q: What is the Khair Contribution subscription?**
A: The Khair Contribution is an optional subscription that allows you to contribute directly through the app.

**Q: How are in-app purchases processed?**
A: We use RevenueCat to securely process in-app purchases. For details on how your purchase data is handled, please refer to RevenueCat's privacy policy.

### Notifications

**Q: How do notifications work?**
A: The app uses local notifications to remind you of your Hawl date and Zakat obligations. All notification scheduling happens on your device.

**Q: Can I customize notifications?**
A: Yes, you can manage notification settings in the app's preferences.

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open or Crashes

1. **Restart your device** and try opening the app again
2. **Check your app version** - Update to the latest version from your app store
3. **Clear app cache:**
   - **iOS:** Settings > General > iPhone Storage > My Nisab > Offload App, then reinstall
   - **Android:** Settings > Apps > My Nisab > Storage > Clear Cache

### Gold/Silver Prices Not Updating

1. **Check your internet connection** - The app needs internet to fetch real-time prices
2. **Restart the app** - Close and reopen the application
3. **Try again later** - If the price API is temporarily down, prices will update once service is restored

### Lost My Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data loss typically occurs when:
- Uninstalling and reinstalling the app (without backup)
- Clearing app data/cache
- Device reset

**Prevention tips:**
- Regularly write down your calculations
- Take screenshots of important data
- Reinstall carefully without clearing app data

### Notifications Not Working

1. **Check notification permissions:**
   - **iOS:** Settings > Notifications > My Nisab > Allow Notifications
   - **Android:** Settings > Apps > My Nisab > Permissions > Notifications
2. **Ensure Hawl date is set correctly** in the app
3. **Restart your device**

### In-App Purchase Issues

1. **Check your internet connection**
2. **Verify your payment method** is valid
3. **Contact RevenueCat support** if the issue persists
