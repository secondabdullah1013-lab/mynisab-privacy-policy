# My Nisab Privacy Policy

My Nisab is a Zakat calculator app for Muslim users. The app helps users calculate their annual Zakat obligation based on their wealth portfolio (gold, silver, cash, trade goods, agriculture, and livestock).

## Data Storage

All wealth data entered by the user is stored exclusively on the user's device using local storage. No personal financial data is transmitted to any server or third party. The app does not require account creation or login.

## Real-Time Price Data

The app uses real-time gold and silver price data fetched from a public API to calculate the Nisab threshold. No user-identifiable information is included in these requests.

## In-App Purchases

The app uses RevenueCat to process in-app purchases (optional Khair Contribution subscription). RevenueCat may collect purchase-related data as described in their own privacy policy.

## Notifications

Local notifications are used to remind users of their Hawl date and Zakat obligations. Notification scheduling happens entirely on-device.
