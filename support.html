Frequently Asked Questions

What is My Nisab?

My Nisab helps Muslim users calculate their annual Zakat obligation based on their wealth portfolio and Nisab eligibility.

Do I need an account?

No. My Nisab does not require account creation, registration, or login.

Where is my data stored?

All wealth and calculation data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your financial information on our servers.

How is Nisab calculated?

The app uses current gold and silver prices to determine Nisab thresholds and calculate Zakat eligibility.

What wealth categories are supported?

Gold, silver, cash, trade goods, agriculture, livestock, and other Zakat-applicable assets.

How do notifications work?

My Nisab uses local notifications to remind you of Hawl dates and Zakat obligations. Notification scheduling occurs entirely on your device.

What is the Khair Contribution?

Khair Contribution is an optional in-app subscription that helps support ongoing development of My Nisab.

Troubleshooting

App not opening?

Restart your device and ensure you are using the latest version of My Nisab.

Prices not updating?

Check your internet connection and reopen the app. Gold and silver prices require internet access to update.

Notifications not appearing?

Verify that notification permissions are enabled in your device settings.
